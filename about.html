About Me

Hi, I'm Thando. I'm 20 years old currently residing in Pretoria. I make cartoons as a hobby and I am a student currently studying software development.

I started making animations 7 years ago. I had wanted to know how to make shows similar to the ones I watched as a child. Shows like Ben 10, Danny Phantom, Chowder and many more shows are what inspired me.

The past few years I've learnt, practiced and shaped my skills or digital art and animation and it has become something I am passionate about.

Aside from art and animation, I am a multi-skilled individual. My skills extends to web development and 3D work as well which gives me a different perspective on environments andthe work I do. My goal is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of creativity and technology.
